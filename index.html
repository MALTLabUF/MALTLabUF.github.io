The Mission

Intersections are critical junctures in urban traffic networks, frequently identified as hotspots for traffic-related injuries and fatalities due to their complex vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) and pedestrian-4 to-vehicle (P2V) conflict points. This study presents a comprehensive case study focusing on5 the utilization of video analytics and artificial intelligence (AI) to enhance intersection safety6 in Broward County, Florida. By leveraging traffic cameras and AI-based methods, the research7 aims to measure and track pedestrian and vehicle movements, providing crucial insights into8 traffic behaviors and potential risks.

Accomplishments

TRB Poster Presentation

We are excited to share that our research, "That Was Close: Video-Based Safety Assessment of Signalized Traffic Intersections along a High-Speed Corridor," has been published in the TRB Annual Meeting Online Program Archive. Presented at the 2025 TRB Annual Meeting in Washington, DC, this work showcases how video analytics and AI can enhance traffic safety and address critical conflict points. This publication enables our findings to reach a global audience, advancing innovative transportation safety solutions.

Featured News Article

The South Florida Sun Sentinel recently featured our research in a compelling article highlighting how artificial intelligence is being used to enhance traffic safety in Florida. The piece details our efforts to leverage AI-driven video analytics at high-risk intersections, demonstrating the potential of innovative technology to reduce crashes and save lives on busy roadways.
